At CosplayAI, we prioritize data minimization. We do not want to hold your data forever. We have designed our system to be transient, meaning your photos are strictly temporary.

1. The 30-Day Auto-Deletion Policy

Automatic Data Wipe: Your privacy is our priority. Uploaded images are never stored, and only the generated results are temporarily kept in your history. All generated content is automatically and permanently deleted from our servers after 30 days, with no backups retained.

2. How We Handle Your Images

When you use CosplayAI (Outfit Replace, Face Edit, etc.):

  • Private Processing: Your image is processed securely by Google Gemini solely to fulfill your specific editing request. It is not shared publicly.
  • No Model Training: Data processed via the Gemini API is not used to train Google's AI models.
  • Temporary Storage: We store the result in your private "My Creations" gallery only so you have time to download it.
  • Isolation: Your files are stored in a private cloud container linked specifically to your User UID.

3. User Control

You do not have to wait 30 days. You have full control:

  • Manual Deletion: If you delete an image from your Gallery inside the app, it is instantly destroyed from our servers.
  • Account Deletion: If you delete your account, all your data (credits, history, uploads) is wiped immediately.

4. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard providers to secure your temporary data:

  • Google Gemini / Vertex AI: The AI engine used for image processing. Google adheres to strict enterprise data privacy standards for API usage.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure, encrypted cloud storage during the 30-day window.
  • Stripe: Handles payment processing. We never see or store your financial details.
